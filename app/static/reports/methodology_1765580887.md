## 2. Databases
- PubMed (Primary): The search was conducted primarily on the PubMed database, which is a comprehensive database of biomedical literature.

## 3. Parameters
- Date Range: The search was limited to studies published in the last 5 years to ensure that the results are up-to-date and relevant.

## 4. Criteria
- Inclusion: The search included clinical trials, reviews, and case reports to capture a range of study designs and methodologies.
